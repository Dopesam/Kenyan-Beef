STEAK

The ribeye steak is a succulent
and flavorful cut of meat taken from the rib section of a cow. This area,
thanks to minimal movement, boasts wonderfully tender meat with generous
marbling, those beautiful streaks of fat you see throughout the steak.

WINE

Different grapes have distinct
flavor profiles. For example, Cabernet Sauvignon is known for its
full-bodied tannins and black fruit notes, while Pinot Noir is lighter
with notes of red fruit and earth.
